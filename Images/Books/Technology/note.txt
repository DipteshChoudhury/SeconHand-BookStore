the soul... :-
author :-Tracy Kidder
The Soul of a New Machine is a non-fiction book written by Tracy Kidder and published in 1981.
 It chronicles the experiences of a computer engineering team racing to design a next-generation computer at a blistering pace under tremendous pressure.
 The machine was launched in 1980 as the Data General Eclipse MV/8000
